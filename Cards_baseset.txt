
Adventurer - 6 gold - Action
	Reveal cards from your deck until you reveal 2 treasure cards. 
	Put those treasure cards into your hand and discard the other revealed cards. 
	
Bureaucrat - 4 gold - Action Attack
	Gain a silver card.
	Put it on top of your deck. 
	Each other player reveals a victory card from his hand and puts it on hi deck. (Or reveals a hand without victory cards).
